Welcome to Paradigm Alaya

Now experience a world of grandeur at Paradigm Alaya - a residential address at Oshiwara. A part of the 102 Downtown township - it offers lavish 1, 2, & 3 BHK apartments with world-class interiors & bath fittings. 40 amenities add to your luxury living here, and include a swimming pool, poolside cafe, party lawn, zumba studio, gym, & more! The property is a mix of serenity & luxury, offering a true downtown-style living in Mumbai.

Based near SV Road at Oshiwara, the property is close to the top-notch landmarks in Mumbai. These include Infiniti Mall, NESCO Business Centre, & Kokilaben Dhirubai Ambani Hospital. The Western Express Highway, D N Nagar Metro Station, & Ram Mandir Station are all within a 10 to 15-minute drive, offering quick travel times as well. Overall, Paradigm Alaya is your entry to an elegant lifestyle right in the heart of the city.

About Paradigm & Prozone

Unveiled in 2015, the founding stone of Paradigm Realty was a blend of industry experience, vision, and sheer enthusiasm. Paradigm personifies contemporary architecture mixed with cutting-edge technology to curate homes that cater to modern and pragmatic needs with nonpareil aesthetics, minuscule floor-space waste, designated functional areas, and more. Everything in custom-designed budgets. Nearing a decade of its legacy, Paradigm is transitioning the fate of Mumbai's real estate, Paradigm Realty operates in prominent locations like Bandra, Santacruz, Khar, Borivali, Andheri, Oshiwara, Thane, etc.

Prozone Intu has been set up to create, develop and manage world-class regional shopping centers and associated mixed-use developments pan-India. Prozone Intu has 15.54 mn sq. ft. of fully paid-up land bank in prime locations with 2.02 mn developed till date and more than 13.5 mn sq. ft. being developed in different phases.
